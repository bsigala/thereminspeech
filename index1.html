How to Use This Builder

Follow these steps at your own pace. Each section builds on the one before it. Click around, explore, and remember that your voice is the heart of this project.

  1. My Story: Start by writing a personal connection to your topic. Use the writing prompt button if you need a spark.
  2. Research & Sources: Log every article, book, or video you consult. The research prompt button offers search ideas and reminds you to diversify your sources.
  3. Outline Skeleton: Add your main points and subpoints. Keep them balanced and distinct so your audience can follow along.
  4. Fill in Details: Flesh out each section with paragraphs, evidence, and examples. If you’re stuck, click the suggestion button for ideas.
  5. Transitions & Flow: Write sentences to bridge your ideas. Use the transition prompt button for sample phrases.
  6. Works Cited: Enter your sources in MLA format. Citation tips help you remember the rules.
  7. Practice & Performance: Rehearse your speech in parts and as a whole. Track your practices, reflect on progress, and try the challenges. It’s normal to feel nervous; practice builds confidence【656297846993776†L116-L139】.
  8. Evaluation & Feedback: Use the rubric above to self‑assess or understand how you’ll be graded. Focus on effort and engagement more than perfection.

You’re not alone in this process. Each tool and prompt is designed to support you on your journey.
